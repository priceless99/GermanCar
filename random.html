BMW I8

Hybrid Supercar

Overview

The BMW i8 truly is a redefinition of what a supercar can be. With its staggering good looks that resemble that of a bedroom poster, including its hugging proportions and butterfly doors, this is BMW’s most striking car ever. The performance of this hybrid car is brutal too, with a 4.4 second 0-62mph sprint, while the handling us up there with the very best sports cars in the game.

Progressive features

Plug-in hybrid power is the way of the future.

Uniquely designed wheels made only for the i8.

The first to design laser-powered headlights

Stunning Design

Brilliant design evokes an emotional response. With their precise lines and sweeping silhouettes, scissor doors and streamflow design, long wheelbases and short overhangs, the i8 models appear to be surging forward even when standing still. Metallic exterior finish with Frozen Gray highlights.

Interior

Open an i8’s doors and you’re greeted by a cabin that is at once extremely beautiful yet, if you’re used to seeing inside BMWs, reassuringly familiar. Just as it should be, then: special yet also entirely usable.

Awaiting the future

BMW offers a single powertrain in the i8, and it's a peach. However, with less power than many competitors, it's not the fastest supercar on the block: The roadster we tested last sprinted from zero to 60 mph in 4.1 seconds. The three-cylinder plug-in hybrid powertrain is smooth and exceptionally well executed, offering a glimpse of an automotive future we can dig. The trio of power sources combines for a total of 369 horsepower, capable of driving all four wheels.
